Basic ATK

Skill

Inflicts a single target enemy with a Foretold status. Deal Quantum DMG equal to 180% of Cipher's ATK to a single target enemy and any adjacent targets.

Ultimate

Deal Quantum DMG equal to 300% of Cipher ATK to a single target enemy. At the same time, deal True DMG equal to 450% of the recorded value of Wealth Bond to the target enemy and any adjacent targets, clearing the recorded amount. When there are more than 1 enemies affected by this attack, distribute the damage equally.

Talent

When there are no enemies inflicted with Foretold on the field when an ally's turn starts or when an ally takes action, Cipher will inflict a random enemy with Foretold, prioritizing the target with the highest HP. Foretold only takes effect on the most recent target it is applied to.

When an enemy with Foretold receives DMG, record 80% of the amount of DMG to the Wealth Bond. True DMG cannot be recorded to the Wealth Bond.

After Cipher's teammates attack an enemy with Foretold, Cipher immediately launches a Follow-up ATK, dealing Quantum DMG equal to 220% of Cipher's ATK to the target enemy. This effect can trigger 1 time(s) and the trigger count resets after using Ultimate.
